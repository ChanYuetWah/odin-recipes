Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian pasta dish made of flat sheets of pasta layered with sauces, cheeses. I learnt about this dish from Garfield, did you? Move on to learn how to make a lasagna!

Total time:

1 hour

Servings:

12 slices

Ingredients

  • 500g lasagna pasta sheets
  • 500mL diced tomatoes
  • 700mL crushed tomatoes
  • 250mL tomato paste
  • 500g Italian style sausages
  • 500g ground beef
  • 500g ricotta cheese
  • 250g mascarpone cheese
  • 120g parmesan
  • 1 shredded mozarella cheese
  • 1 tsp parsley flakes
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ large diced onion
  • 3 chopped garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp oregano
  • 1 tbsp basil
  • 1 pinch nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add chopped garlic, minced sausages and beef. Season with salt, pepper and parsley. Let cook well until the beef is well done.
  2. Put a saucepan to medium fire to fry garlic and chopped onions in olive oil. Stir to mix all the ingredients, cover the saucepan and let cook for 10 mins. Add chopped tomatoes and tomato paste when the chopped onions are golden and soft. Season with salt, pepper, oregano and basil.
  3. Mix the sauce and the meat in a pan and save for later.
  4. Mix ricotta, mascarpone, parmesan cheese, eggs, salt, pepper, and nutmeg in a bowl thoroughly.
  5. Boil lasagna sheets in a pot for 60 to 90 seconds. Transfer to a bowl of cold water and place them on parchment paper and dry with a paper towel. Repeat until all the lasagna are cooked. **Put a sheet of parchment paper between each lasagna sheet so that they do not stick.
  6. butter the baking pan and pour a layer of tomato sauce on the bottom of the pan. Then, create a layer of pasta.
  7. Spread a thick layer of creamy cheese on top of the pasta. Then, create a thick layer of the ground meats mixture, spreading wvwnly with a spoon. Cover the meats sprinkling mozzarella generously on top.
  8. Repeat building the layers until runnng out of ingredients.
  9. On top, add a layer of pasta covered with tomato sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  10. Butter a sheet of aluminum foil and cover the lasagna with it, leave some free space on top. Make several punctures into the aluminum foil anf take the lasagna into the oven for 45 mins at 180°C.
