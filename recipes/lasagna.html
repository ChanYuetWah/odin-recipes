Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian pasta dish made of flat sheets of pasta layered with sauces, cheeses. I learnt about this dish from Garfield, did you? Move on to learn how to make a lasagna!

Total time:

1 hour

Servings:

12 slices

Ingredients

  • 500g lasagna
  • 500mL diced tomatoes
  • 700mL crushed tomatoes
  • 250mL tomato paste
  • 500g Italian style sausages
  • 500g ground beef
